Project Overview

A normalized relational database designed to manage core hospital operations, including patient registration, appointment scheduling, treatment tracking, and billing systems.

Core Tables

  • Patients: Demographics, contact info, insurance details
  • Doctors: Medical staff, specializations, departments
  • Appointments: Scheduling system with status tracking
  • Treatments: Medical procedures and interventions
  • Billing: Financial transactions and insurance claims

Sample Query: Patient Appointment History

SELECT 
    p.FirstName, p.LastName, 
    a.AppointmentDate, 
    d.FirstName AS DoctorFirstName,
    d.Specialization
FROM Patients p
JOIN Appointments a ON p.PatientID = a.PatientID
JOIN Doctors d ON a.DoctorID = d.DoctorID
WHERE p.PatientID = 1
ORDER BY a.AppointmentDate DESC;
